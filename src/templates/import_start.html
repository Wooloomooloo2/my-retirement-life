Choose your My Financial Life file

My Retirement Life can read your My Financial Life data file (.mfl) to set up your accounts, investments, property and budget — so you don't have to type them in again. Your file is read only; nothing is written back to it, and nothing leaves your device.

What comes across

  • Cash & investment accounts, with current balances
  • Property & vehicles (as physical assets)
  • Loans → repayment budget lines; your budget categories

What you'll still add in MRL

Your retirement profile, income, drawdown order & tax, and life events — the forward-looking parts MFL doesn't hold. You'll also set a growth rate for each investment (MFL tracks past returns, not forecasts).
